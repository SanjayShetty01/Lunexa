Lunexa

Arbitrage & Hedge Calculator

Lunexa is a precision-focused betting calculator designed to help you identify and manage risk-free opportunities using real market odds. Whether you are analysing odds before placing a bet or locking in profit after odds move, Lunexa gives you clear, math-driven answers.

Pre-Bet Arbitrage

Before placing any bets, Lunexa checks whether the available odds across different outcomes create an arbitrage opportunity. When one exists, it calculates the exact stake distribution needed to guarantee profit, regardless of the outcome.

Post-Bet Hedging

If you have already placed a bet and the odds change later, Lunexa helps you determine whether you can hedge to break even or secure a guaranteed profit. You can set your goal; break-even, percentage profit, or a minimum profit amount; and Lunexa will calculate the required hedge.

Lunexa does not predict outcomes or future odds. All calculations are transparent and based solely on the numbers you provide.
